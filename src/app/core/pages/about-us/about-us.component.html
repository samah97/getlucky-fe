About us

The auction platform for everyone.

Welcome to Get Luck, a platform born out of passion and expertise, created by two driven individuals with a shared love for technology and innovation. Meet Samah Daou and Raafat Waheb, Lebanese expatriates who brought their skills to the Netherlands, and united by a common vision to revolutionize the online bidding experience.

Our Story

Samah and Raafat, both seasoned developers, embarked on a journey to develop Get Luck from the ground up. Their inspiration stemmed from a desire to infuse excitement and accessibility into the world of online auctions. Drawing from their diverse backgrounds and experiences, they meticulously crafted this platform to offer a seamless, user-friendly, and engaging bidding experience.

What Sets Us Apart

What distinguishes us is not just the functionality of our platform, but the passion and dedication poured into every line of code. We value user feedback and continuously work to enhance the bidding process, ensuring a secure and enjoyable experience for all participants.
