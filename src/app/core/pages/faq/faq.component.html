01 How does bidding work on GetLuck?
Bidding is simple! Once you've registered and logged in, find a product you like, enter your bid amount, and hit "Place Bid." Wait for the lottery and good luck!
02 Is there a limit to the number of bids I can place?
No, there's no limit! You're welcome to place as many bids as you'd like on different items.
03 Can I retract a bid once it's placed?
Unfortunately, bids cannot be retracted once they're submitted. Make sure to bid responsibly and only on items you genuinely intend to purchase.
04 How long do auctions typically last?
Each item listing has a draw date and time, ranging from a few days to several weeks in the future. The lottery draw date is set based on engagement forecast and estimates. Please note that we may have to postpone the lottery in case the number of participants is too few. This ensures fairness and randomness.
05 Are there any hidden fees beyond the winning bid?
No hidden fees! The winning bid amount is the final price you'll pay, excluding shipping costs if applicable.
06 Is my personal information secure on your platform?
Absolutely. We take your privacy seriously and employ robust security measures to safeguard your information.
07 How do I know if I've won the lottery?
You'll receive an email if you've won the item! Additionally, you may see your first name listed on the winners page :)
08 What forms of payment do you accept?
We currently accept secure online payments via credit/debit cards or other designated payment methods specified at checkout.
